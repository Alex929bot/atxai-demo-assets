Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

Prepared for: ATX AI Solutions

This Business Associate Agreement ("Agreement") is made between ATX AI Solutions ("Business Associate") and the Covered Entity ("Client"). This Agreement sets forth the terms under which the Business Associate will create, receive, maintain, or transmit Protected Health Information (PHI) on behalf of the Client.

1. Definitions

Terms used in this Agreement shall have the same meaning as those terms in HIPAA (45 CFR Parts 160 and 164).

2. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

Business Associate may use or disclose PHI only as necessary to perform services for the Client and as permitted by this Agreement and HIPAA.

3. Safeguards

Business Associate shall implement administrative, physical and technical safeguards that reasonably and appropriately protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of PHI.

4. Breach Notification

Business Associate shall report to Client any use or disclosure of PHI not permitted by this Agreement, including breaches of unsecured PHI, as required by law.

5. Subcontractors

Business Associate shall ensure that any subcontractor that creates, receives, maintains, or transmits PHI on behalf of the Business Associate agrees to the same restrictions and conditions that apply through this Agreement.

6. Termination

Upon termination, Business Associate shall return or destroy PHI per Client's direction and as required by HIPAA.
